      Scanning a QR code is a straightforward process and will provide all detailed information to farmers or customers. We will be providing two dynamic QR codes so that the farmer will get the information related to the product, and another one is useful for the traceability of the product. We also cover farmers who don't have smartphones to scan QR by providing a calling bot option.For this we will be providing a unique number for each product and also a tracking ID will be provided for tracing their order.
